About “Mama I’m a Billionaire”

“Mama I’m a Billionaire” isn’t just a name it’s a quiet promise. It’s for the days when nothing feels certain, but you keep going anyway. It’s about believing that one day, all the long nights, risks, and sacrifices will turn into something real. Not just success for yourself, but a moment you can share—when you finally look your mom and dad in the eyes and say it, not as a dream, but as the truth you built.

- Lukas Mieliauskas
